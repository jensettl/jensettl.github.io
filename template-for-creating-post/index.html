    Add a specific header to your post

    After creating the markdown file for your post, add the following header to the top of the file.

    ---
layout: post
title:  "Title of your post"
author: jens
categories: [ Category 1, Category 2 ]
tags: [ Tag 1, Tag 2 ]
rating: 4.5
image: path/to/image.jpg
beforetoc: "Short paragraüh to add additional information to your post"
toc: true # Summary
---

    Content

    After the header start writing the content of your post in markdown syntax.
